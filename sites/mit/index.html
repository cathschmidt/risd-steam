MIT STEAM wants to unite artists and engineers.

About Us

MIT STEAM is a student-led effort to ignite communications between disparate fields in academia, business, and thought. Our focus is broad but our starting point is uniting the Arts with STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics).

Our current project is a Wintersession/IAP workshop series between RISD, Brown, and MIT. It is called HUMAN+COMPUTER.

Get Involved

Anyone is welcome, regardless of your major or background. We think that a diversity of backgrounds is essential to our success. Of course, don't hesitate to email us anytime at info@steamwith.us. We can't wait to hear from you!

See our companion studios: RISD, Brown.

If you are interested in starting a chapter of STEAM at your university or organization, please visit steamwith.us/new.
